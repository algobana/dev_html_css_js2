Feel the Spirit of the City

İstanbul Sokak Lezzetleriyle Bir Gün

Morning: Simit & Tea

Start your day like a true local — with a crispy simit and a glass of strong Turkish tea, preferably on a ferry ride.

Noon: Roasted Chestnuts

Follow the smoky scent in the streets — warm roasted chestnuts are the perfect cozy treat on chilly noons.

Afternoon: Kokoreç

Bold, spicy, and grilled to perfection — kokoreç is a lunchtime favorite for adventurous foodies.

Night: Stuffed Mussels

End the night on the streets with lemon-squeezed stuffed mussels. One is never enough.
